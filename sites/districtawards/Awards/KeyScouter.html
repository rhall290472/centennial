Key Scouter Award

This award recognizes a "Special" adult in your unit (Pack, Troop, Ship, and/or Crew) who stays behind the scene to make Scouting happen. This person is the one who can always be counted-on at the last minute to help make things happen successfully in the unit. This is the person in your unit who always has a smile and is always enthusiastic about everyone and everything.
